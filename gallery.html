Every year, with a new edition of IQMU, we strive to break barriers and provide you, with the most enriching medical quizzing experience possible.
From Manipal, to the vibrant cities of India, to the world, witness the fruits of our labour from our previous contestants
Dr. Vinay Khanna
Faculty Advisor, IQMU

" IQMU hosted every year by KMC,Manipal provides unique Interactive platform to medical students across the world to test their knowledge, understand competition and to hone their teamwork skills. It is one of the unparalleled medical quizzing experience that all medical students must experience at least once. I’m so proud to be affiliated with Team IQMU. The efforts of the organising team in finessing the quiz rounds, and showcasing workshops by student clubs like TEMPUS, JUSTITIA, CUTTING EDGE is a joy to witness. They help deliver a well-rounded experience to all participating teams, especially the increasing number of International Teams every edition. A multi-day extravaganza wherein students around the globe interacts, exchange ideas and achieve accomplishments. IQMU is the event to be at!. I wish great success and all my best to the incoming teams, and the organising committee for their upcoming edition. "
Dr Vinod Nayak
Faculty Advisor, IQMU

" It has been a great pleasure to watch IQMU grow and evolve with each passing year, and to watch more and more students actively participate and engage with each other in this one-of-its-kind experience. Medical quizzing is not only an opportunity for budding doctors to broaden their horizons and interact with their peers, but also helps them to grow into well-rounded clinicians. As IQMU 2021 makes it's foray into the online platform, the organizing committee has put in a tremendous amount of effort to bring together a quiz that provides a holistic approach to medicine and it's practice, with challenging rounds and interactive workshops. IQMU 2021 will indeed be an enriching experience for both it's national and international participants, and I look forward to being a part of it's ever-evolving journey! "
